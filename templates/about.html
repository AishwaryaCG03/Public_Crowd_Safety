About CrowdSafe

A comprehensive crowd disaster prevention and management system

Our Mission

CrowdSafe is dedicated to preventing crowd disasters and enhancing safety at events of all sizes. Our comprehensive system combines real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and emergency response tools to help event organizers manage crowds effectively and respond quickly to potential issues.

Key Features

Predictive Bottleneck Analysis

Our AI-powered system monitors crowd density and movement patterns in real-time, identifying potential bottlenecks before they become dangerous. The system provides immediate notifications to organizers when potential crowd disasters are detected.

Incident Reporting & Response

Report various emergency situations including medical emergencies, security issues, and other critical incidents. The system automatically simulates nearby response units and provides organizers with optimal dispatch recommendations.

Intelligent Evacuation System

In emergency situations, our system helps individuals navigate to safety by showing the shortest and safest exit routes when they enable location sharing, with special zoom functionality for venue-specific mapping.

Lost Person Detection

Our sophisticated system allows caretakers to report missing individuals, generating immediate notifications for organizers. Using AI-powered analysis of camera and drone footage, the system can detect missing persons through facial recognition, clothing patterns, and other identifiable features.

Geofencing Alert System

Monitor restricted areas and receive instant notifications whenever unauthorized personnel enter these zones, ensuring complete venue security and crowd control.

AI Chatbot Assistant

Our AI chatbot allows organizers to ask specific questions about any zone, receiving detailed descriptions of current bottleneck situations and predictive analytics for what might happen in the next 20 minutes.
